## Who are you?

Not sure where to start? Pick the path that fits:

| You are a... | Start here |
|---|---|
| **Trader** wanting to swap tokens | [CoW Protocol](/cow-protocol/explanation/introduction/intents) — understand intents, then [place a swap](/cow-protocol/tutorials/cow-swap/swap) |
| **Developer** integrating CoW into your app | [CoW SDK](/cow-sdk/quickstart) — quickstart, then [widget](/cow-protocol/howto/integrate/widget) or [API](/cow-protocol/howto/integrate/api) |
| **Solver operator** running a solver | [Solvers](/cow-protocol/tutorials/solvers/create) — create, test, and deploy |
| **Contributor** to the codebase | [BFF & Services](/bff/introduction) — architecture and dev setup |
| **DAO participant** or grant applicant | [Governance](/governance/explanation/mission) — mission, process, and [how to apply](/governance/howto/how-to-apply) |
